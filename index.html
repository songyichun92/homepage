Yichun Song (宋依纯)

I am currently a fifth year PhD student in the department of Economics, the Ohio State University, where I work on Empirical IO and Applied Econometrics. My advisors are Professor Adam Dearing, and Professor Matthew Weinberg.

Before studying at OSU, I attended Vanderbilt University from 2014 to 2016, and got my Master's degree in the Graduate Program of Economic Development (GPED). My Bachelor's degree is obtained from the School of International Trade and Economics, University of International Business and Economics (UIBE), 2014, Beijing, China.

I am on the job market this year (2021-2022), and I am available for interview at ASSA 2022 Boston. I am planning to graduate at the end of Spring 2022.
